Planning and Monitoring.</p></Title><ShortDescription><p>Business analysis is the first and a vital stage of any complex IT project. This uncertainty makes the job of a business analyst interesting, but significantly complicates planning and further management of business analysis process. That’s why it is very important to pay due attention to competent planning and management at this project phase. </p><p>Business analysis planning and management issues are covered in detail by Business Analysis Body of Knowledge® (BABOK®).
